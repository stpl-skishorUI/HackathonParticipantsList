Room Allocation Rules :
  1. Rooms are subject to availability.
    Allocation will be made on a first-come, first-served basis or as per the approved schedule.
  2. No double booking.
    A room can be allotted to only one team or meeting at a time.
  3. Priority usage.
    • Senior management cabins (e.g., RK Sir Cabin, Abhay Sir Cabin, Sachin Sir Cabin) require prior approval before booking. (In case they are using their cabin)
    • Common rooms like Conference A, Conference B, and Studio Cabin are open for general team use.
  4. Booking duration.
    Each booking slot must be limited to the predefined meeting durations (e.g., 45 minutes).
  5. Punctuality.
    Teams must start and end meetings on time to ensure smooth transitions for the next booking.
  6. Cleanliness & Responsibility.
    The allocated team is responsible for maintaining cleanliness and ensuring that the room is left as it was found.
  7. Equipment care.
    Any devices (projector, screens, cables, etc.) used in the room should be handled carefully.
    Damages must be reported immediately to the IT or Admin team.
  8. Cancellation policy.
    If a meeting is canceled, the room should be released in the system at least 15 minutes before the scheduled time. You can inform other team owners, in case they can occupy the same.
  9. Right to reassign.
    The Admin or Management team reserves the right to reassign or modify room allocations in case of priority requirements or maintenance.
